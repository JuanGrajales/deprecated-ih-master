Netlify Setup

  1. Head to Netlify.
  2. Click on the sign up button located on the upper right corner.
  3. Sign up using GitHub by clicking on GitHub (if you do not have GitHub account stop here and make a GitHub account first).
  4. Enter your GitHub credentials.
  5. Click on Authorize netlify for GitHub.
  6. Click on Next.
  7. Click on Next.
  8. Click on Finished.
  9. Click on New Site from Git on the upper right corner.
  10. Click on GitHub under Continuous Deployment.
  11. A new window will open, authorize Netlify once more.
  12. Click on Install (make sure to leave All respositories checked).
  13. The window will close and you will now be on step 2. Choose the repository you want to deploy.
  14. You will now be on step 3. Finish deploying by clicking on Deploy site.
  15. You will be taken to the Overview page and Netlify will take a couple of minutes to finish deploying.
  16. Once Netlify finishes you will see the link turn from yellow to green.
  17. Pat yourself on the back for a job well done.
